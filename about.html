About Project

Predictive Analytics for Secure Mobile Transactions

The rapid evolution of digital finance has escalated the challenges associated with detecting and preventing financial fraud. Traditional fraud detection systems, relying heavily on methods like Deep Neural Networks (DNNs), Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs), and Stochastic Gradient Descent (SGD), often struggle with issues of opacity and centralized data handling, which can compromise both interpretability and data privacy. In response, the integration of Explainable AI (XAI) and Federated Learning (FL) offers a promising solution. XAI enhances the transparency of fraud detection models, making their decisions more interpretable and trustworthy. Concurrently, Federated Learning addresses privacy concerns by enabling a decentralized approach to model training, where insights are gleaned without exposing individual data points.

Future Enhancements:
Integrating with the mobile payment apps and making the transactions as decentralized.
